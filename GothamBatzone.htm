GothamBatzone

Gotham: A city shrouded in darkness, crowded with the most notorious criminals in history and protected by a tortured anti-hero.
Batman’s fictional home is just one of several areas that’ll be part of The Gotham Batzone. Gotham Batzone is a brand new theme park that opens this summer in California. It will show a glimpse of the world of Batman.

Batman: Knight Flight

The highlight is going to be a ride called “Batman: Knight Flight,” which takes guests on a trip into the Batcave and then on a twisty, turny, appropriately dark ride as they test Batman’s new “ultimate urban patrol vehicle.”

The Batcave Museum

A time line for all the batman movies, and props.

Joker's Funhouse

A Joker related funhouse in which people will encounter and actively interact with various devices designed to surprise, challenge, and amuse the visitor.

Hall of Doom

An interactive 3D show with a tour of Wayne Towers and a performance from the Flying Graysons!

Scarecrow Scare Raid

A thrilling water roller coaster which will have jump scares.

Riddler Revolution

A 100ft. Ferris wheel which is fun for sight-seeing.

Arkham Cafe

Coffee Cafe that has the Nightwing expresso!

Mr. Freeze Ice Scream Truck

A stop-and-go truck which will serve special frozen shaved ice cream!
